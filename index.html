This Privacy Policy explains how Roomwise ("Roomwise", "we", "us") handles your information when you use the Roomwise mobile app. Roomwise helps you plan a room and check whether furniture fits, with AI-assisted suggestions from our in-app assistant, "Nova". By using Roomwise, you agree to the practices described here.

Roomwise is provided by [YOUR NAME OR BUSINESS NAME]. You can reach us at [YOUR CONTACT EMAIL].

1. Information we collect

  • Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and a password, and optionally a display name. Passwords are handled by our authentication provider (Google Firebase Authentication) — we never see or store your password ourselves.
  • Room photos you choose to upload. When you ask Nova to analyze a room, the photos you select are sent for processing to generate suggestions (see Section 3). We do not store your photos on our servers after processing.
  • Room details you enter. Room dimensions, furniture pieces, layouts, doors, and windows you add in the planner, along with saved analyses. This information is stored locally on your device.
  • Limited usage data. To prevent abuse and manage costs, we keep a simple per-account counter of how many AI analyses you run each day (a date and a count linked to your account). We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising identifiers, or third-party trackers.

We do not collect payment information, contacts, precise location, or browsing history, and Roomwise does not contain third-party advertising.

2. How we use your information

  • To create and secure your account and sign you in.
  • To process the photos and room details you submit and return AI-generated design and fit suggestions.
  • To enforce a reasonable daily limit on AI requests.
  • To respond to your support requests and keep the app working reliably.

3. AI processing and third-party services

Roomwise relies on a small number of service providers to function. We do not sell your personal information to anyone.

  • Google Firebase provides our authentication, backend functions, and a small database used for the usage counter described above. Your account information is stored with Firebase. See Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.
  • OpenAI processes the room photos and room details you submit in order to generate Nova's suggestions. These are sent to OpenAI's API through our secure server and are used only to produce your results; they are not used to train models through OpenAI's API, and are subject to OpenAI's data policies. See https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy.

4. Where your information is stored and how long we keep it

  • Account information is retained by Firebase for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, it is removed.
  • Room details and saved analyses are stored on your device and remain there until you delete them, delete your account, or uninstall the app.
  • Room photos are processed to generate suggestions and are not retained on our servers afterward.
  • The daily usage counter is a minimal record tied to your account and is not used to identify you beyond rate-limiting.

5. Your choices and rights

  • Delete your account at any time. In the app, go to Profile → Delete account. This permanently deletes your account and clears the room data stored on your device. This action cannot be undone.
  • Access and correction. You can update your display name in the app, and you can contact us at [YOUR CONTACT EMAIL] with questions about your data.
  • Regional rights. Depending on where you live (for example, the EU/UK under GDPR, or California under the CCPA/CPRA), you may have additional rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information. To exercise these, contact us at the email above and we will respond as required by applicable law.

6. Children's privacy

Roomwise is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Access to AI processing requires a signed-in account, and the credentials used to reach our AI provider are kept on our server, not in the app. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above, and significant changes may be highlighted in the app. Your continued use of Roomwise after an update means you accept the revised policy.
